The Physical Product
Smarter than
your smart home.

The U·Dump Sensor Kit attaches to any standard toilet seat in under 10 minutes. No plumbing. No drilling. No grossness. Just load cells, Bluetooth, and competitive glory.

U·Dump Sensor Ring

⚖️
Load Cell Array
±0.1 lb precision

Four HX711-based load cells sit inside a slim ABS ring that fits between your toilet seat and bowl. No tools required — standard wing-nut mounting. Weighs you before, weighs you after. The math handles itself.

📡
BLE 5.0 + ESP32
30ft range

The brain lives inside the ring itself — an ESP32 microcontroller paired with BLE 5.0. No hub. No dongle. No Wi-Fi password required. Communicates directly to your phone. Your data goes straight to the leaderboard. Where it belongs.

🔋
Battery Module
6-month life

Rechargeable lithium cell array. Six months per charge. Magnetic USB-C — just snap the cable to the bottom of the ring. No disassembly. No excuses. IP54 splash-rated because obviously.

We built the most advanced toilet seat sensor in history. Then we asked ourselves: what if we kept going? The U·Throne Pro adds Bluetooth audio, precision heated water, seat warming, and a vibration motor — all synced to your session data. Your body deserves the best environment in which to perform.

🔊
Spatial Audio System
5W driver

A 5-watt waterproof speaker built into the seat housing. Syncs to your phone via Bluetooth. Plays your Dump Playlist™ automatically when a session begins. Plays the U·Dump victory fanfare when you break a record. Cannot be turned off during record-breaking sessions. This is by design.

🌡️
Thermal Comfort Suite
5 heat levels

Heated seat surface (37°C–42°C) with five precision levels. Heated water bidet with adjustable pressure, angle, and temperature. Pre-warms 30 seconds before your scheduled sessions based on historical Dump Score™ patterns. Your body knows when it's time. So does the seat.

📳
Victory Vibration Engine
haptic feedback

Dual eccentric motor array delivers haptic feedback events timed to in-app achievements. Subtle pulse on session start. Escalating rumble when approaching a personal record. Full celebration mode on throne claims. Your friends feel nothing. You feel everything. That is the point.

🤖
DumpMind™ AI Coach
Pro exclusive

Encouraging real-time AI feedback delivered through the speaker during your session. DumpMind™ monitors your duration, weight trajectory, and historical data to provide live, personalized, deeply sincere motivational commentary. Because everyone deserves a coach.
